Features and Services

Chat Box

Our chatbox enables students and educators to communicate in real time. They can ask and answer questions, discuss course topics, and share resources.

Zoom Integration

With Zoom Integration , we enable educators to conduct live online classes and meetings so students can interact with educators in real time virtually.

Upload Documents

A document portal where educators can upload course materials such as lectures, notes, and assignments. Students can access these materials and use them to enhance their learning.
